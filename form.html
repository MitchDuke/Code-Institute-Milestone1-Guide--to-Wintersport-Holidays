What Our Visitors Have to Say About Us

Tour operators

Always book with a tour operator, it will potentially cost you more but the added security and having a rep on site to help resolve any issues is invaluable, especially if the native language is not your own.

Winter sports Insurance

Always check that your insurance covers you for winter sports, because a lot of accident or standard travel insurance does not cover you, ensure it has emergency return flight cover so in the unlikely event that you are injured, you can get home.

Equipment

If it is your first time always hire your equipment at the resort, once you decide you like the sport you should then look into your own equipment. It is one less thing to arrange to take with you and one less thing that can go missing at the airport. If you take your own equipment remember to allow for the extra baggage.
