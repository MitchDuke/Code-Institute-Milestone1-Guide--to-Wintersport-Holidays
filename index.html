Five Reasons to go on a winter Holiday

To feel healthy

The act of Snowboarding or Skiing is great exercise, it burns calories and gets the metabolism moving. The clean air can also help clear your respiratory system. It can lower your cholesterol and help you lose weight.

To be social

Not to be underestimated is the social aspect of winter holidays, you can meet new people on and off the slopes and develop friendships with people with similar interests who you would never have met in your day-to-day life.

Because its fun!

Winter sports put you right in the centre of gorgeous scenery, and whether you like the rush of speeding down the slopes, doing tricks in one of the many freeriding parks or the chilled out trek through tree lined vistas there will be something for everyone.

To feel freedom

The feeling of freedom out on the mountain is like no other, fresh air, clear skies. It has many physical and mental health benefits.

Feel Stronger

It can strengthen your heart and reduce blood pressure. Being a weight bearing exercise it will strengthen your bones and increase muscle tone, help improve endurance, balance, coordination, and improve overall health.

Things to Consider when taking your first winter holiday

Tour operators

Always book with a tour operator, it will potentially cost you more but the added security and having a rep on site to help resolve any issues is invaluable, especially if the native language is not your own.

Winter sports Insurance

Always check that your insurance covers you for winter sports, because a lot of accident or standard travel insurance does not cover you, ensure it has emergency return flight cover so in the unlikely event that you are injured, you can get home.

Equipment

If it is your first time always hire your equipment at the resort, once you decide you like the sport you should then look into your own equipment. It is one less thing to arrange to take with you and one less thing that can go missing at the airport. If you take your own equipment remember to allow for the extra baggage.
